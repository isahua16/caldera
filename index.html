The Rabbit Haul

Find out about our game

The Rabbit Haul is a single-player tower defence farming game with action-roguelite elements where you play as a friendly rabbit gardener protecting its haul of fruits and veggies from waves of hungry raccoons known as the Trash Panda Posse. With an array of unique weapons, upgrade paths, powerful towers and crops to unlock, the game offers countless play styles to master.
